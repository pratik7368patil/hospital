What are chronic wounds?

When regular wound healing processes are disrupted by under-lying diseases, a poorly healing wound (chronic wound) can develop even after a trivial injury. To promote wound healing, it is necessary to diagnose and treat the underlying disease so that normal wound healing conditions are restored.
